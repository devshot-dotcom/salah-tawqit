Platforms supported by Salah Tawqit

Salah tawqit is a free-to-use and community-oriented application that's being developed and maintained by an open-source non-profit organization (for now). The application was developed with the sole intention to serve the Muslim Community in their daily prayer needs and allow anyone anywhere in the world to look-up prayer times for any city/state of the world.

tip

The app is in consistent and continued development since mid 2021, following are the platforms currently supported by the app.


Android#

Android is the first platform that the app was developed for and the initial release was also on the same platform. Find the app on Google Playstore.
