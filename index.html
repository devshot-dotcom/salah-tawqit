Prayer Times For Anywhere

Salah tawqit gives its users the ability to look-up prayer times for any city/state around the world by the click of a button. While this feature requires the user to enter a city and a corresponding country name, one can also adopt the automatic calculation that automatically calculates prayer times for the current device location.
